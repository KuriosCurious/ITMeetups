Blockchain / Crypto

Monadnock Decentralized Currency Network

Keene, NH

If you live, work, or shop in the Monadnock region, you're welcome to join! Whether you are new to decentralized currencies, (such as Bitcoin, DASH, Bitcoin Cash, etc) or an experienced user, you are welcome. We regularly meet to help the people and businesses in the region to adopt decentralized currency, increase stores of wealth, and promote the local economy.

(Web 3.0) Distributed Internet Platforms

Manchester, NH

This is a group for people with experience in computer programming, website development, and or digital media. This is a group for people who want to learn and collaborate on a blockchain based project.

Information Security

InfoSec or Die

 Bedford, NH

Southern New Hampshire meetup group for information security professionals, hobbyists, researchers, and everything in-between. Come join us to talk and exchange ideas over drinks.

PineSec

 Manchester, NH

PineSec is an informal InfoSec meetup, to discuss topics of interest in a non-work, non-vendor setting. PineSec meets the 1st Wednesday, in Manchester, NH.

UNH Cyber Center

Durham, NH

This meetup is for anyone interested in contributing to building the cyber talent of the future. Security practitioners, community leaders, researchers, and students are all welcome to join. The long term goals for the Center include: 1) Positioning UNH as a critical regional cybersecurity resource. 2) Facilitating real-world learning experiences for students. 3) Providing industry access to students interested in cybersecurity. The intent of this meetup to identify and implement activities to help the Center reach its goals. Events, student projects, mentoring, and community outreach.

Programming

Granite State-NH .NET

Salem, NH

Calling all .NET developers looking for inspiration! All things .NET (Framework, Core, UWP) are converging to become the intersection of all your favorite computing form factors (desktop, laptop, tablet, smartphone, command line, service, and web app), and your favorite development tools (especially C# and Visual Studio). Come get tips, tricks, tools, and INSPIRATION that can help you get your idea off the drawing board and successfully into your users' hands.

New Hampshire Python Group

Manchester, NH

This is a group for people interested in Python, improving their skills, or even those who advanced in the language. All skill levels are welcome, and we look forward to meeting other people with our passion for Python development.

New Hampshire WordPress

Manchester, NH

We are a community of people -- designers and programmers, experts and aspirants, volunteers and business people, citizens and public servants -- who gather to share our knowledge and experience, our successes and challenges, our war stories and adventures in creating websites with WordPress. Our goal is to help each other develop awesome, engaging, useful and, yes, revenue-generating sites. Our community is open to all who love WordPress or who want to learn more about it. Come join us, have some fun, share what you know, learn from others, and help guide where we go with this group and what we do.

nh.js: New Hampshire's JavaScript User Group

Manchester, NH

If you're using, have used or are generally interested in the world of JavaScript and Web Development, this is the group for you! We're JavaScript enthusiasts that are interested in meeting other like-minded enthusiasts. Each monthly meeting includes an interesting topic for an interactive discussion, refreshments and networking time. Our goal is to add value to the JavaScript world. We want to hear from you about topics of interest or if you have a desire to speak at a meeting.

NH node.JS Meetup Group

Nashua, NH

Group for Node.JS enthusiasts and professionals. All skill levels welcome.

Queen City Sitecore User Group

Manchester, NH

The Queen City Sitecore User Group meets monthly in Manchester, NH for networking, learning - and did we mention free food? The user group is open to anyone using or considering the Sitecore Experience Platform, or who has an interest in .NET CMS platforms, regardless of technical proficiency. Programmers, marketers, content authors, CMS users and others are all welcome. We will be discussing all aspects of the platform from logistics to programming, and will have guest speakers on occasion.

SeacoastSQL User Group

Portsmouth, NH

This is a FREE group where you can learn about all aspects of the Microsoft Data Platform, from on-premises SQL Server and the cloud data offerings from Microsoft in Azure and even using SQL Server in AWS. We can have topics that cover administration, development, BI, ETL, and big data solutions.

Upper Valley Web Developers

Lebanon, NH

Do you have a love for web development, or are you interested in learning what it's all about? Whether you use it to create web applications or software to power knitting machines and cat food dispensers, this is the place to come share your passion and get inspired, or event just gripe about The Bad Parts.
